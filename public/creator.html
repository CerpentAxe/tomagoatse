Use the on-screen pad and buttons under the preview. Keyboard shortcuts work if you plug in a keyboard.

  • W A S D or arrows — move (forward / left / back / right)
  • Space — jump (on the ground)
  • Ctrl — duck (hold)
  • H — hide / show
  • F — toggle flight · Space / Ctrl — rise / descend while flying (wings only)
  • Pad — walk · Jump / Duck / Hide
  • Fly — toggle flight · Rise — hold while flying (wings)
